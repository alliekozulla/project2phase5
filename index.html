ANOVA - Monthly Income

P-value: <.0001​, R2: .8224​, Monthly income with gender and attrition had p-value: < 0.001. However, R2 was less than 0.03.,​ Monthly income with attrition, gender, job role continued the statistical significance, and significantly increased the R2​
